New York City

Experience the magic of NYC! From it's iconic skyline and bustling streets to its diverse neighborhoods and endless entertainment, New York City is a place like no other. Whether you're exploring world-class museums, savoring street food, or enjoying the nightlife. The city that never sleeps!

Empire State Building

The Empire State Building is known for the beautiful view over the city, and attracts millions of visitors each year. The price to visit is 86th & 102nd floor: 87$ adult, 80$ children(6-12), free children(under 6), 84$ seniors (62+)

Time Square

Discover the heart of NYC at Times Square! This iconic destination is bursting with bright lights, entertainment, and energy. From world-famous Broadway shows to stunning digital billboards, it's the place where excitement never stops. Whether you're here to shop, dine, or just soak in the vibe, Times Square is the ultimate experience that truly defines New York City!

Central Park

The most popular park in the world. In central Park u can walk around and explore the beauty of it. You can also visit the zoo, You can row a boat and ice skate in the wollman rink which is open from october-april.

Nets basketball Game

Catch the excitement of watching a Brooklyn Nets game in NYC! Feel the energy of the crowd, the thrill of each play, and the buzz of live action at Barclays Center. Whether you're a die-hard fan or just love the atmosphere, it's the perfect way to experience New York sports at its best!
